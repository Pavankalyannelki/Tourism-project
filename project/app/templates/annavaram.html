ANNAVARAM

Detailed information about the place.

  • ABOUT

    Annavaram is quite a picturesque destination with the Pampa River flowing languidly next to the hills. Opposite the main shrine is the kalyana mandapam. Other shrines in the temple premises are dedicated to Vana Durga and Lord Rama. Initially, the temple was a mere shed built over the idol of Satya Narayana, which was found here in 1891. Over time it was constructed into the structure you see today. The main temple is in the shape of a chariot with four wheels.Read moreRead less
